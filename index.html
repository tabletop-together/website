Beginners Character Creation

Neverwinter Wood lvl 3

A beginner Friendly one-shot

You are on a mission to recover a powerful magical item lost within some ruins found in Neverwinter Wood

Freedom by Siege lvl 5

The party awaken to find themselves in a jail cell deep in a castle. Screams and yells echo the halls as its currently under siege by an invading force. An explosion rocks the adjoining cell wall from a ballista, The two cells now connected, a door open...this is their chance, freedom or death. No matter your backgrounds, in this they are united. You will need to navigate the halls, avoid possible sentry and find a way out.

Death in Space

The Tenebris Solar System: once a teeming hub for humanity, now home to a handful of broken worlds in a collapsing universe. DEATH IN SPACE is a game about surviving in an abandoned frontier where resources are scarce, and competition is fierce
